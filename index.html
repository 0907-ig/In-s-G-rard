Adidas History

Adidas is a German company founded in 1949 by Adolf Dassler, specializing in the manufacture of sporting goods, and based in Herzogenaurach, Germany. It is known worldwide as "the brand with the three stripes," referring to the three parallel stripes that make up its logo.

Adidas SL 720

With their retro design, the adidas SL 72 OG are gradually becoming the new obsession of fashionistas. Spotted on the feet of the most followed it-girls, these sneakers seem ready to invade our wardrobes this winter.

Superstar

The adidas Superstar first appeared on a basketball court in 1970. Its immediate success quickly made it a true icon of urban style. This legendary shoe has since become a superstar in the world of trends and fashion. It has been worn by numerous celebrities and influencers who are now fashion icons. It has been a go-to shoe in the music scene, among sneakerheads, and in streetwear.

Campus

Although not the most famous of the brand's three-stripe sneakers, this model is a must-have, just like the Samba, for example. Recently, we also witnessed its timeless appeal with the somewhat surprising arrival of its 00s version. The adidas Campus 00s has indeed become a must-have for both men and women.

Adidas gazelle cow

With their eye-catching cow-print design, the adidas Samba steps out of its comfort zone and straight into the spotlight. Already a staple in minimalist wardrobes, this unexpected twist gives the silhouette a fashion-forward upgrade. Equal parts nostalgic and experimental, these sneakers are quickly becoming a favorite among those who dare to stand out.

Samba

Originally designed for indoor football, the adidas Samba has effortlessly transitioned into a streetwear essential. With its sleek silhouette and understated design, it has become a go-to for those who appreciate timeless style. Worn by everyone from athletes to fashion insiders, the Samba continues to prove that simplicity never goes out of fashion.

Gazelle

With its clean lines and vintage charm, the adidas Gazelle has long been a quiet icon in the sneaker world. Recently rediscovered by a new generation, it’s making a strong comeback in the wardrobes of fashion lovers everywhere. Its soft suede upper and understated silhouette give it a refined yet relaxed vibe that fits perfectly with today’s trends.
